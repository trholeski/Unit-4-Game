Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking any of the crystals you will add a specific but unkown amount to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randmon number. You loe the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game restarts , after winning or losing, the random number as well as the value of each of the jewels changes.

Good luck!
